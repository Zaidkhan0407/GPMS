Q: What is this application about?
A: This application is designed to help users find job opportunities and improve their resumes.

Q: How do I search for jobs?
A: You can search for jobs by entering keywords related to the job title, company, or skills in the search bar.

Q: How do I upload my resume?
A: You can upload your resume in the profile section of the application.  Supported formats are PDF and DOCX.
